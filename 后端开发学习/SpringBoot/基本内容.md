对于SpringBoot有**xml，properties和yml**的配置方法
```YAML
● properties:
server.port=8080
server.address=127.0.0.1
● xml:
<server>
  <port>8080</port>
  <address>127.0.0.1</address>
</server>
● yml:
server:
  port: 8080
  address: 127.0.0.1
```
